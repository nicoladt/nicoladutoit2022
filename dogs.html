My awesome dogs

Congratulations! If you've landed on this page it means that you found the easter egg in my CV.

Behold! The majesty that is my beautiful and ridiculously silly Rough Collies: Sophie and Bailey.

I am also employed by three gorgeously quirky cats - Django, Scarlett and Ada - who are far too elusive and full of disdain to be concerned with the internet.

Sophie

Sophie is almost 15 years old. She is our most gifted child with several titles to her name: Flyball Champion (FCH), Flyball Dog Excellent (FDX), and KUSA Gold Canine Good Citizen. Sophie is retired now, but she still enjoys catching tennis balls, napping in the sun and barking at hadedas.

Bailey

Bailey is our pupper - he is 3 years old. He enjoys tummy rubs, chewing sticks and taking naps on the couch.
