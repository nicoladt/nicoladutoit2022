My awesome dogs

Congratulations! If you've landed on this page it means that you found the easter egg in my CV.

Behold! The majesty that is my beautiful and ridiculously silly Rough Collies: Sophie and Bailey.

I also own three gorgeously quirky cats - Django, Scarlett and Ada - who are far too elusive and mysterious to be shared with the internet.

Sophie

Sophie is almost 12 years old. She is my most gifted child with several titles to her name: Flyball Champion (FCH), Flyball Dog Excellence (FDX), and KUSA Gold Canine Good Citizen. Sophie is retired now, but she still enjoys catching tennis balls, napping in the sun and barking at hadedas.

Bailey

Bailey is our babydog - he is less than 1 year old. Bailey is working hard at dog school, learning to sit and walk nicely on a lead. Bailey likes face scritches and chewing sticks and stealing shoes out of the cupboard.

In memoriam: Dexter (2010 - 2019)

Dexter was our most special, cuddly boi. He passed away very suddenly in February 2019. There will always be a Dexty-shaped hole in our hearts.
